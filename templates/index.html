About GeoCities

GeoCities was a web hosting service founded in 1994, which allowed users to create and publish their own websites. It was one of the earliest services that provided easy-to-use website creation tools, making it accessible for people with little to no coding experience to build their own personal or professional web pages.

Historical Significance

In the late 1990s and early 2000s, GeoCities was incredibly popular, hosting millions of websites. It played a crucial role in the democratization of the web, offering a platform for self-expression and community building. At its peak, GeoCities was the third most visited website on the internet.
